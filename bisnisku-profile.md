# Bisnisku — Business Profile & Strategy

## Company Overview

**bisnisku.info** adalah platform SaaS all-in-one untuk bisnis offline di Indonesia. Platform ini menggabungkan business directory modern (Yellow Pages 2.0) dengan suite management tools yang di-power oleh AI. Didirikan oleh solo founder dengan background business/marketing, Bisnisku di-build dengan pendekatan revenue-first sebelum mengejar venture capital.

## Vision & Mission

**Vision**: Menjadi platform #1 di Indonesia yang membantu jutaan bisnis offline bertransformasi digital.

**Mission**: Menyediakan tools yang powerful namun mudah digunakan — dari marketing, CRM, loyalty, sampai payment — dalam satu platform terintegrasi yang di-optimize oleh AI.

**Tagline**: "Partner Cerdas untuk Pertumbuhan Bisnis Anda di Era Digital"

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams (Hybrid)

1. **Subscription Revenue (Primary)**
   - 5 tiers: Free → Starter (Rp999K) → Growth (Rp2.9M) → Business (Rp8.9M) → Enterprise (Rp18.9M)
   - Monthly billing via Xendit recurring
   - Feature-gated upsell dari Free ke paid tiers

2. **Transaction Fee Revenue (Secondary)**
   - Starter: 3% per transaksi
   - Growth: 1.5% per transaksi
   - Business: 0.5% per transaksi
   - Enterprise: 0% (included)

3. **Future Revenue Streams** (post-launch)
   - Bisnisku Points subsidy settlement (50% dari merchant)
   - Premium directory placement / featured listings
   - White-label / custom integrations untuk Enterprise

### Unit Economics Target
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): < Rp500K (organic via directory + GBP claim)
- LTV (Lifetime Value): > Rp12M (12+ months retention × avg Rp1M/month)
- LTV/CAC Ratio: > 24x
- Monthly Churn Target: < 5%
- NPS Target: > 50

## Market Analysis

### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
- 64 juta+ UMKM di Indonesia (BPS 2023)
- ~8 juta bisnis offline di kategori target (F&B, Beauty, Health, Automotive)
- Jakarta alone: ~500K+ bisnis offline eligible

### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
- Bisnis offline urban yang aware digital: ~2 juta
- Willing to pay for SaaS tools: ~200K

### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM) — Year 1
- Jakarta pilot: 5,000 directory listings, 1,000 paid merchants
- MRR target: Rp500M-1B (Month 12)

### Competitive Moats
1. **Data Moat**: GBP directory → organic merchant acquisition tanpa sales team
2. **AI Moat**: AI bukan add-on, tapi embedded di setiap fitur
3. **Network Effect**: Bisnisku Points cross-merchant → user lock-in
4. **Switching Cost**: All-in-one = high switching cost setelah merchant adopt

## GTM (Go-To-Market) Strategy

### Phase 1: Jakarta Pilot (Month 1-6)
- Import 5,000+ GBP listings ke directory
- Launch city directory: bisnisku.info/jakarta
- WhatsApp outreach ke top 500 bisnis (salon + restoran)
- Target: 200 claimed listings, 50 paid merchants

### Phase 2: Jakarta Growth (Month 6-9)
- Scale outreach ke semua vertikal
- Content marketing: SEO blog, social media
- Referral program: merchant refer merchant
- Target: 1,000 paid merchants, MRR Rp300M

### Phase 3: BODETABEK Expansion (Month 9-12)
- Replicate Jakarta playbook ke Bogor, Depok, Tangerang, Bekasi
- GBP import per kota
- Local partnership (komunitas UMKM, asosiasi bisnis)
- Target: 3,000 paid merchants, MRR Rp1B

### Phase 4: National Scale (Year 2+)
- Top 10 kota Indonesia
- React Native mobile app launch
- Enterprise partnerships
- Potential VC funding round

## Risk Register

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|
| Solo founder burnout | High | Claude Code as force multiplier, prioritize ruthlessly |
| Low merchant adoption | High | Free tier + GBP claim = low-friction entry |
| WhatsApp API policy change | Medium | SMS fallback via Twilio, email as secondary |
| Google Places API cost spike | Low | 5-layer cost filter, $200/month free credit |
| Competitor copy features | Medium | Speed + AI moat + network effect |
| Payment fraud | Medium | Xendit fraud detection + manual review threshold |
| Data privacy (UU PDP) | High | RLS multi-tenant, data encryption, privacy by design |
